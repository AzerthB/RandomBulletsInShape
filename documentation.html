Warhammer 40k applet to randomize artillery & firearm bullets in a tabletop area.


Usage :

Set preferred zoom for your screen type in the top left

Place ruler on your screen and adjust the scale at the
left of the bottom bar to match your ruler length

Choose the number of bullets and their impact diameter

Choose the wanted shape of the affected zone

Bullets will automatically fire every time a button
is pressed and the list of positions for every one
of them will be on the top right

You can then easily measure the same distances in
real life using your ruler (measured from the bottom left)
and then check which characters are affected by the projectiles

There is also a silhouette mode meant for single enemies
or boss fights
